The USAF Transition Challenge

Translating Air Force AFSC experience into civilian language is the single biggest obstacle veterans face during transition. Civilian hiring managers and the ATS systems they use cannot interpret AFSC codes, rank abbreviations, or military jargon — which means qualified veterans get filtered out before a human ever sees their application.

The solution is translation. Not summarizing what you did — but genuinely converting your duties, qualifications, and accomplishments into the civilian language that both ATS systems and hiring managers understand and respond to.
