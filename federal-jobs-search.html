Veteran Job Search Tips

Veterans Preference
When you apply through USAJobs, your 5-point or 10-point preference automatically adds to your score. Learn how preference works →
Federal Resume Required
Federal resumes are 3-5 pages minimum. A standard civilian resume will be rejected. AI Federal Resume Builder →
Know Your GS Grade
Your rank and experience map to a GS grade. Applying to the wrong grade wastes time. GS Grade Estimator →
Federal Hiring Takes Time
Federal hiring averages 3-6 months. Apply to multiple positions simultaneously and search across all 5 platforms above.
Clearance = Higher Pay
An active TS/SCI clearance adds $10K-30K to your salary. Don't let it lapse. Clearance salary data →
Translate Your MOS
Use civilian keywords in your resume, not military jargon. "Platoon Sergeant" becomes "Operations Manager." MOS Translator →
