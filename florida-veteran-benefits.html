🌴 State Benefits

Florida Veteran Benefits Guide

No state income tax. Property tax discounted by your disability percentage. 100% P&T gets full exemption. Florida's warm climate and veteran-friendly tax environment make it a top relocation choice.

View All State Benefits

Florida is consistently ranked as one of the top states for veterans, combining no state income tax with strong property tax exemptions, excellent VA healthcare access, and a large military community across multiple installations. These benefits are separate from federal VA benefits and require separate application.

Property Tax Exemptions

Veteran StatusExemption
Any veteran with service-connected disabilityDiscount on property taxes equal to disability percentage (e.g., 70% rating = 70% discount)
100% permanent and total disabled (P&T)Full property tax exemption on primary residence
Surviving spouse of qualifying veteranFull property tax exemption (continued after veteran's death)
Veterans 65+ with combat disabilityFull property tax exemption

Apply through your county property appraiser with DD-214 and VA award letter. The partial exemption (percentage-based) is one of the most veteran-friendly structures in the country — a 70% disability rating gives a 70% property tax discount, which on a $350,000 home saves approximately $4,000-$6,000 per year.

Income Tax Benefits

Florida has no state income tax. Military retirement pay, VA disability compensation, civilian wages, and investment income are all completely tax-free at the state level. Combined with the property tax exemptions, Florida is one of the most tax-advantaged states for veteran retirees.

Education Benefits

Florida offers several veteran education programs at the state level:

  • Florida Veterans Vocational Rehabilitation: State-funded vocational rehabilitation for veterans with disabilities, separate from federal VR&E.
  • Waiver of Tuition Fees for Children of Deceased Veterans: Children of veterans who died from service-connected causes may receive tuition waivers at Florida public universities.
  • Priority Registration: Florida public universities provide priority registration to veteran students.
  • Gold Card Discount: Many Florida community colleges offer reduced-cost continuing education to veterans.

Vehicle and License Plate Benefits

Florida offers free specialized veteran license plates (one per vehicle). Veterans with a service-connected disability also receive free registration on one vehicle annually. Over 50 specialized veteran plate designs are available, many with designation of specific service branch or award. Apply through the Florida DHSMV.

Other Key Benefits

Florida Department of Veterans Affairs

Free claims assistance, nursing home care, and transition assistance. myflorida.com/FDVA — statewide offices at most county veterans service offices.

Free Help
State Veterans Nursing Homes

Six state veterans nursing homes with priority admission and significantly subsidized rates for eligible veterans.

State Employment Veterans Preference

Florida law requires public employers (state, county, city agencies) to give veterans preference in hiring. Extends to surviving spouses of veterans who died from service-connected causes.

Professional License Reciprocity

Florida has agreements with several states for license reciprocity for military spouses and veterans. Check DBPR.myflorida.com for specific professions.
