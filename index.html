What Is Veteran Career Path?

The most comprehensive free veteran resource online.

Veteran Career Path is a complete career transition and benefits platform built by a medically retired U.S. Army Sergeant who experienced the military-to-civilian struggle firsthand. The site exists because TAP alone is not enough — and veterans deserve a single place to find everything they need, explained in plain language, without paying hundreds of dollars for coaching or navigating dozens of disconnected government websites.

With over 800 free resource pages, Veteran Career Path covers every branch of military service across six categories: career transition tools including MOS-to-civilian translators for all 561 military specialties, VA benefits guides with disability calculators and claims assistance, education resources including GI Bill comparisons and certification roadmaps, financial planning tools for retirement and TSP management, state-by-state veteran benefit guides for all 50 states, and mental health resources including a SAMHSA treatment locator by ZIP code. Every one of these resources is completely free — no account required, no paywall, no tricks.

For veterans who want AI-powered career tools, the platform also offers 20+ subscriber tools for $15 per month — including an AI resume builder that pre-loads your military profile, a job match analyzer that scores you against any job posting, an interview simulator with STAR-format coaching, a federal resume builder for USAJobs applications, and a salary negotiation advisor. Every AI tool understands military experience out of the box, so you never have to explain what your MOS means or why your background matters.

Whether you are 12 months from ETS, freshly separated, or a veteran who has been out for years, this platform was designed to meet you where you are. Learn more about our mission and founder.
