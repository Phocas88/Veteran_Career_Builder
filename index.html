Our Mission

"The transition gap is real. We close it."

Veterans leave with some of the most valuable skills in the American workforce — leadership under pressure, accountability, systems thinking, and the ability to operate in chaos. But the civilian world doesn't speak military, and TAP only scratches the surface.

Veteran Career Path was built to be what TAP isn't — a comprehensive, always-on resource that covers the full transition lifecycle: career, benefits, education, finances, legal rights, and mental health. In one place. The resources are free. The AI tools are $15 a month.

The goal is for this to become the platform TAP counselors recommend, VSOs bookmark, and every service member gets handed on their last day.
