Maine Veteran Benefits

Complete 2026 guide to veteran benefits in Maine — home to Naval Air Station Brunswick veterans and Maine Maritime Academy.

🏠 Property Tax Benefits

100% Disabled Veterans

100% P&T disabled veterans may qualify for a full property tax exemption on their primary residence. Contact your county assessor to apply.

Partial Disability Exemption

Veterans with 10%+ VA disability ratings typically qualify for partial property tax exemptions. The exemption amount increases with disability rating.

Surviving Spouse

Unremarried surviving spouses of veterans killed in action or who died from service-connected disabilities often qualify for property tax relief.

🎓 Education Benefits

In-State Tuition

Veterans using GI Bill benefits at Maine public universities qualify for in-state tuition rates regardless of residency period, per the Veterans Access, Choice and Accountability Act.

State Scholarship Programs

Maine may offer state-funded scholarships for children and spouses of veterans killed or totally disabled in service. Check with the Maine DVA for current programs.

National Guard Tuition

Active Maine National Guard members typically qualify for reduced or waived tuition at state universities. Contact your unit education officer.

🎣 Recreation & License Benefits

Hunting & Fishing Licenses

100% disabled veterans in Maine typically qualify for free or reduced-cost hunting and fishing licenses. Check with Maine Fish & Wildlife for current rates.

State Park Passes

Many Maine state parks offer free or discounted admission for veterans and active duty service members. Some require a veteran ID or DD-214.

Vehicle Registration

Veterans in Maine may qualify for free or discounted vehicle registration, specialty veteran license plates, and disabled veteran plates with parking privileges.

🏥 Healthcare & VA Resources

Maine DVA Office

The Maine Department of Veterans Affairs administers state benefits, accredits VSOs, and provides claims assistance. Capital: Augusta.

TRICARE & VA Healthcare

Federal VA healthcare is available to eligible veterans at VA medical centers and community-based outpatient clinics throughout Maine.

Mental Health Resources

Veterans Crisis Line: 988, press 1. The Maine DVA also offers state-funded mental health programs for veterans not eligible for VA care.
