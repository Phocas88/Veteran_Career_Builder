Military Acronyms Civilian Guide

200+ military terms translated into civilian language for your resume, LinkedIn, and cover letters. Stop losing interviews to terminology hiring managers don't understand.

Use MOS Translator Free

Military acronyms are career kryptonite on a civilian resume. What reads as impressive, specific experience to a veteran sounds like an unfamiliar language to a civilian hiring manager. This guide gives you 200+ military acronym translations — use it to clean your resume, LinkedIn profile, and cover letters before any application.

The Rule

If a civilian HR manager would Google it, spell it out. Better yet, replace it entirely with the civilian equivalent. "Conducted PMCS IAW applicable TMs" means nothing to a hiring manager. "Performed preventive maintenance on $2M in equipment per manufacturer specifications" means something.
