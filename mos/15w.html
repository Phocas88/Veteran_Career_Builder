How to Translate 15W on a Resume

Never list "15W" as your job title on a civilian resume. Terms like "15W", "Army", and military rank abbreviations mean nothing to a civilian ATS and will get your resume filtered out before a human sees it.

Wrong way

"15W Unmanned Aircraft Systems Operator, Army - Responsible for execution of duties in accordance with applicable regulations."

Right way

Use the civilian job title, civilian action verbs, and quantify everything. How many people did you supervise? What was the value of equipment you managed? What did you improve or achieve? Veteran Career Path translates this automatically.

GI Bill and Education Options

If you need additional credentials, the Post-9/11 GI Bill (Chapter 33) covers tuition, housing, and books. Veterans with a 20 percent or higher disability rating may qualify for VR&E (Chapter 31), which covers full education costs plus a monthly stipend. The DoD COOL program can cover certifications while you are still on active duty.

Frequently Asked Questions

What civilian job is equivalent to 15W Unmanned Aircraft Systems Operator?

The closest civilian equivalents are UAS/Drone Operator, Aerial Surveillance Specialist, Remote Sensing Analyst. Your specific role will depend on years of experience, additional qualifications, and security clearance level.

How much do 15W veterans make in civilian jobs?

Veterans with 15W backgrounds typically earn $55,000–$110,000 depending on location, industry, and experience. Veterans with active security clearances or advanced certifications often earn at the top of that range.

Do I need a degree to get hired as a 15W veteran?

Not always. Many civilian roles that align with 15W experience value hands-on experience and certifications over degrees, particularly in technical, law enforcement, and operations fields. A relevant degree will open additional doors and increase compensation.

How do I write 15W on a civilian resume?

Replace "15W" with the civilian job title equivalent, describe your duties with civilian action verbs, and quantify your accomplishments. Veteran Career Path's AI resume builder does this translation automatically for you.
