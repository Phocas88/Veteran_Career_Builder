💡 Split Your TLEYou can use TLE at both your old AND new station — up to 10 days combined. Many service members use 3–4 days at old station, 6–7 days at new.
PPM/DITY — Make Money on Your MoveGet 95% of what the government would have paid a commercial carrier. Keep the difference as tax-free profit (within entitlement weight).
StepAction Required
1. AuthorizeRequest PPM from Transportation Office BEFORE moving. Do not move first.
2. Tare WeightWeigh your truck/trailer EMPTY at certified scale before loading.
3. Gross WeightWeigh again when FULLY LOADED. Net = gross minus tare.
4. PaymentGovernment pays 95% of the linehaul cost + fuel surcharge for your net weight and distance.
5. SubmitWeight tickets + receipts to Finance within 45 days of arrival.
PPM Formula (Estimate)Incentive = Net Weight × Linehaul Rate × Distance × 95%. The DoD uses zip-code linehaul tables + fuel surcharges. Our estimate uses $0.17/lb/100mi as an approximation — actual rate from your TMO will differ.
DITY Advance (60%)You can request 60% of estimated PPM payment in advance from your TO before moving. Must be repaid if actual weight is lower than estimated.

Advance Pay (PCS)You may request up to 3 months of base pay in advance for PCS expenses. This is an interest-free loan repaid over 12 months via payroll deduction.
ItemDetails
AmountUp to 3 months base pay (no allowances)
InterestNone — 0% interest
RepaymentSpread over 12 months via payroll deduction
When to RequestBefore PCS — coordinate with Finance
DITY AdvanceSeparate from advance pay — 60% of estimated PPM payment, requested from TO
💡 StrategyUse DITY advance (from TO) to cover truck rental costs before the move. Use advance pay (from Finance) to cover deposits and setup costs at new station. Both are available simultaneously.
⚠️ Do NOT Move Before OrdersMoving before official PCS orders = you pay everything. Verbal or email notice does not count.
1-Year WindowYou have exactly 1 year from your report date to use your PCS move entitlement. After that, it's forfeited permanently.
⚠️ Storage LimitsTemporary storage (SIT): 90 days at origin. Non-temporary storage (NTS): up to 1 year at destination. Excess = you pay.
💡 Partial DITYYou can ship some things yourself (get PPM pay for those) while the government moves the rest. Common strategy: ship valuables yourself, government moves furniture.
POV Shipment (OCONUS)Hawaii, Alaska, and overseas: 1 POV shipped at government expense. Some OCONUS locations authorize 2. CONUS-to-CONUS: not funded (you drive and get MALT, or ship at own cost).
