💻 Remote Careers

Remote Career Paths for Disabled Veterans

High-paying civilian careers that translate directly from your military experience - without the physical workplace requirements. Mapped by MOS with salary data.

Translate Your MOS Free

For veterans with physical disabilities, chronic pain, limited mobility, or conditions that make traditional workplace environments difficult, remote work is not a compromise - it is often the optimal career path. Many of the highest-paying careers that translate directly from military experience are fully remote-capable. This guide maps specific MOS codes to remote career opportunities with salary data.

Key Insight

Remote work eliminates commuting barriers, allows schedule flexibility for VA appointments, enables customized ergonomic setups, and removes many workplace accommodation needs entirely. The best accommodation is often just working from home.

High-Demand Remote Careers by Military Background

Intelligence and Analysis (25 Series, 35 Series, CTI/CTN, 1N7)
Intelligence Analyst (Remote)

Salary: $65,000-$130,000. Translates directly from military intelligence experience. Most cleared contractor roles are remote-eligible. Required: active clearance preferred. Certs: CompTIA Security+, certified intelligence certifications.

Clearance Premium
Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) Analyst

Salary: $60,000-$110,000. Growing field, fully remote. Corporate and government clients. Translates from any ISR, CI, or all-source background. No clearance required for many roles.

Remote-First
Risk and Threat Analyst

Salary: $70,000-$120,000. Financial institutions, consulting firms, corporate security. Fully remote. Leverages military analytical frameworks.

Remote-First
Cybersecurity (25D, 17C, CTN, 1B4, 6206)
Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst

Salary: $55,000-$105,000. Shifts of monitoring and incident response. Fully remote for most employers. Entry-level roles available with Security+ and military background. No degree often required.

Remote-First
Penetration Tester

Salary: $85,000-$160,000. Fully remote in most cases. Requires CEH, OSCP, or similar. High demand, difficult to fill. Military network operations background is directly applicable.

Remote-First
Cybersecurity Consultant

Salary: $90,000-$150,000. Advisory roles with minimal travel. Cleared background commands premium rates. CompTIA, CISSP, CISM certifications valuable.

Clearance Premium
Cloud Security Architect

Salary: $130,000-$200,000. Fully remote, senior role. AWS, Azure, or GCP security specialization. High ceiling, strong demand. Military IT background with additional cloud certs.

High Ceiling
IT and Systems (25B, 25U, IT, CTN, 3D0, 6420)
Systems Administrator (Remote)

Salary: $55,000-$100,000. Managing servers, networks, and infrastructure remotely. Fully remote standard. Military IT experience translates directly. CompTIA Server+, MCSA, or AWS certs helpful.

Remote-First
IT Project Manager

Salary: $75,000-$130,000. Coordinating technology projects. Remote-first at most companies. PMP certification with military PM experience is a strong combination.

Remote-First
Help Desk / IT Support (Tier 2-3)

Salary: $45,000-$75,000. Entry path into IT sector. Fully remote for many employers. CompTIA A+ is the baseline cert. Strong stepping stone to higher IT roles.

Entry Point
Technical Writer

Salary: $60,000-$100,000. Creating technical documentation for software, hardware, and procedures. Fully remote. Military experience writing SOPs and operational documentation is directly relevant.

Remote-First
Program and Project Management (All Branches)
Federal Program Manager

Salary: $80,000-$150,000. Managing government contracts and programs. Hybrid to fully remote. DAWIA or PMP certification. Senior military officers and senior NCOs transition well.

Clearance Premium
Agile Scrum Master

Salary: $85,000-$140,000. Facilitating software development teams. Fully remote. CSM certification. Military operations experience translates well to agile facilitation.

Remote-First
Contract Specialist

Salary: $65,000-$120,000. Federal and commercial contracting. Remote eligible. DAWIA certification for federal. Military contracting backgrounds (51C, AFSC 6C0) translate directly.

Hybrid Available
Finance and Analysis (36A, 36B, Financial Specialists)
Financial Analyst (Remote)

Salary: $60,000-$110,000. Budgeting, forecasting, financial reporting. Most are remote-eligible. CFA or CPA certification opens senior roles. Military finance background is strong prep.

Hybrid to Remote
Budget Analyst (Federal)

Salary: $65,000-$110,000. Federal agencies. Remote or telework-eligible GS positions. Veterans preference applies. CGFM certification valued.

Federal Telework

Building Remote Work Into Your Negotiation

Many veterans with disabilities don't ask for remote work even when they need it because it feels like a special request. It isn't. As of 2025, fully remote and hybrid roles are standard across most knowledge work sectors. In technology and cybersecurity, remote-first is the default expectation.

If a role is listed as in-office but you need remote work as an ADA accommodation, you can request it through the reasonable accommodation process. Many employers who say "in-office preferred" will accommodate remote work for a qualified candidate with a documented need.
