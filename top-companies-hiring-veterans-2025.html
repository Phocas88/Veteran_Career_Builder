How to Approach These Companies

Find Their Veteran ERG First

Search "[Company Name] veteran ERG" or "[Company Name] military employee resource group." Connect with ERG leaders on LinkedIn before applying. Warm connections outperform cold applications every time.

Look for SkillBridge Partnerships

Many of these companies are official SkillBridge partners. You can do a 6-month fellowship at your target company while still on active duty and paid. Check DoD SkillBridge's official partner directory.

Use Their Veteran-Specific Job Boards

Most large companies on this list have a military-specific landing page on their careers site. Use it. These applications often route to a military-focused recruiter rather than the general ATS queue.

Reference Their Veteran Commitments

In your cover letter: "I noticed [Company] committed to hiring 100,000 veterans and has an active ERG" shows research and signals cultural fit to military hiring managers.

Match Your MOS to These Companies

The Job Match Analyzer compares your military profile to specific job postings at any company. Subscribers get AI-powered analysis of fit and gap areas.
