How to Choose Your Path

Match to Your MOS

Your military specialty is the strongest predictor of which certification path will be fastest. A 25B (IT specialist) has a 12-month head start on cybersecurity certs. A 68W (medic) is halfway to paramedic or nursing. Use our MOS Translator to see which industry aligns.

Time vs. ROI

Entry-level certs (A+, Security+, CNA) take 1-3 months and open immediate employment. Mid-level certs take 3-6 months but often double starting salaries. Senior certs require experience prerequisites - build toward them while working.

Fund It First

Before paying out of pocket: check VR&E eligibility, GI Bill applicability, employer tuition assistance programs, and free options (DAU, federal training programs). Many of these certifications cost nothing if you use the right benefit.
