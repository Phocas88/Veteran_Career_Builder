Most veteran job searches start at job boards. That is the wrong place to start. Before applying to any of these companies, find veterans who already work there on LinkedIn and request a 15-minute informational conversation. An internal referral from a fellow veteran increases your callback rate by 40-60% compared to a cold application - even at companies with strong veteran programs.
