Infantry / Combat Arms (11 Series, 0311, 11X)

BEFORE (Military Language)
• Led 9-man rifle squad during OIF III rotation
• Conducted presence patrols and cordon and search operations
• Maintained PMCS on assigned crew-served weapons
AFTER (Civilian Language)
• Supervised and developed a 9-person team in a high-pressure, mission-critical environment across a 9-month overseas deployment
• Planned and executed 200+ security operations with zero personnel casualties
• Maintained 100% readiness of $2M+ in specialized equipment

Full Summary Block - Infantry Sergeant

PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY
Combat-proven Operations Leader with 8 years of U.S. Army service including three overseas deployments. Proven track record managing personnel, equipment valued at $2M+, and mission execution under extreme pressure. Earned Army Commendation Medal for superior performance in leadership. Expert in team building, risk assessment, security operations, and multi-team coordination. Seeking operations or management role leveraging military leadership expertise.

Logistics / Supply (92A, 92F, 68J, 2S0, SK)

BEFORE
• Performed PBO duties for battalion
• Conducted inventories IAW AR 710-2
• Processed ULLS-S4 transactions daily
AFTER
• Managed property accountability for $18M in organizational equipment with zero discrepancies across 3 consecutive annual inventories
• Processed 150+ daily supply transactions using enterprise inventory management systems
• Reduced supply request turnaround time by 40% through process improvement initiatives

Information Technology (25B, 25U, IT, 3D0)

BEFORE
• Maintained NIPR/SIPR networks for battalion
• Performed IA duties as ISSO
• Managed IAVA patching per IAVM requirements
AFTER
• Administered and secured classified and unclassified network infrastructure supporting 500+ end users across 3 locations
• Served as Information Security Officer (ISO), ensuring compliance with DoD cybersecurity frameworks
• Managed monthly vulnerability patching cycle, reducing critical vulnerabilities by 94% within 30-day windows

Healthcare (68W, HM, 4N0, 18D)

BEFORE
• Provided TCCC and ATLS interventions downrange
• Maintained BAS operations with 97% medical readiness
• Conducted sick call screening for 800-person battalion
AFTER
• Delivered emergency and primary medical care to combat casualties in austere environments, maintaining a 100% survivability rate among patients reaching care
• Managed medical readiness for 800+ personnel, maintaining 97% readiness rate — highest in brigade
• Conducted daily patient intake, assessment, and treatment planning at battalion aid station

Intelligence (35 Series, CTI, CTN, 1N7)

BEFORE
• Produced HUMINT collection requirements
• Conducted PIR analysis in support of BCT operations
• Maintained SIPRNET database of SALUTE reports
AFTER
• Developed intelligence collection requirements and directed human intelligence assets in support of brigade-level operations
• Produced daily analytical products synthesizing open-source and classified reporting for senior decision-makers
• Maintained classified intelligence databases, ensuring integrity and accessibility for time-sensitive operations
